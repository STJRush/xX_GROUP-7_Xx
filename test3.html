History

Tesla used to collaborate with NVIDIA to make the information system.

Tesla's new model is the Model 3.

Model 3 achieves 220 miles of range while starting at only 35,000 USD before incentives.

Tesla is now collaborating with Intel to make the Infotainment system.

The Infotainment system will have a touch screen and will be easy to use.

The Tesla model 3 will have an autopilot mode.

Tesla initially wanted the Model 3 to be dubbed the Model E, but that dream was dashed when Ford filed for the Model E trademark.

In addition to the Model Y, Tesla wants to produce a mini bus and semitruck in the future.
