Tesla Model S

Model S is designed from the ground up to be the safest sedan on the road. With unparalleled performance delivered through Tesla's unique, all-electric powertrain, Model S accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in 2.5 seconds. Model S comes with Autopilot designed to make your highway driving stress free.
