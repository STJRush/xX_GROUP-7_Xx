Here are a few facts about the Tesla Model 3

Interesting information

1. Model 3 achieves 220 miles of range while starting at only 35,000 USD before incentives.

2. Tesla is now collaborating with Intel to make the Infotainment system.

3. The Infotainment system will have a touch screen and will be easy to use.

4. The Tesla model 3 will have an autopilot mode.

5. Tesla initially wanted the Model 3 to be dubbed the Model E, but that dream was dashed when Ford filed for the Model E trademark.

6. In addition to the Model Y, Tesla wants to produce a mini bus and semitruck in the future.

7. It will be extremely safe

8. It can go 0-60 MPH in less than 6 seconds
